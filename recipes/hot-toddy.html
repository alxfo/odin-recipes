Hot Toddy

Description

A warming cure for what ails you, or simply to see you through a cold winter night

Ingredients

  • One shot scotch whisky
  • 1tsp freshly squeeze lemon juice
  • 1tsp runny honey
  • Three dried cloves
  • One cinnamon stick
  • 50ml boiling water

How to make

  1. Pour the whisky, lemon juice and honey into a mug
  2. Stir
  3. Add the cinnamon stick and cloves
  4. Pour the boiling water into the mug
  5. Stir again
  6. Garnish with a slice of lemon
